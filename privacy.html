Website Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This policy applies to visitors of handoverengine.com only. If you are a user of the Lastday platform, a separate Application Privacy Policy governs your use of that service.

1. Who We Are

Handover Engine Incorporated is a Canadian corporation headquartered in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. We operate the website at handoverengine.com. Questions about this policy may be directed to info@handoverengine.com.

2. What This Website Collects

When you visit handoverengine.com we may collect the following information automatically:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Date and time of your visit

If you submit a contact form, book a demo via Calendly, or send us an email we will collect your name, email address, and any information you choose to provide in your message.

3. How We Use This Information

We use the information collected from this website to understand how visitors find and use the site, to respond to enquiries and demo requests, to improve the website and its content, and to prevent fraud and technical issues. We do not use your information to serve third party advertising.

4. Cookies

This website uses cookies to understand visitor behaviour and improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit the site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies, required for the website to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies, used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site. All data is anonymised and aggregated.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the website. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Third Party Services

This website uses the following third party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics, anonymous visitor traffic analysis. analytics.google.com
  • Calendly, demo booking. calendly.com
  • GoDaddy, website hosting. godaddy.com

We do not sell or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Data Storage

Visitor analytics data is processed by Google Analytics and stored on Google servers. Contact form submissions and demo booking information are stored securely and accessed only by the Handover Engine team. Our servers are located in Canada.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you, to request correction of inaccurate information, and to request deletion of your information. To exercise any of these rights please contact us at info@handoverengine.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Links to Other Websites

This website may contain links to third party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies directly.

9. Minors

This website is not directed at persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at handoverengine.com. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the website after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
