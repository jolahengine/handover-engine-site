Common questions

The questions everyone asks
before booking a demo.

How long does it actually take an employee to complete their handover?+
About 20 minutes for most roles. The flow is structured and guided. The employee answers one question at a time, uploads their files, and the AI does the heavy lifting. They don't sit in front of a blank document. We ask. They confirm. The AI builds it.
What if the employee refuses to complete their handover?+
The manager dashboard shows amber and red status in real time. Nudges can be sent directly from the dashboard. Critically, the document exists from day one. It fills in over time. Even a partially completed handover is dramatically better than nothing. And the dignity-first design means most employees want to complete it. Their work mattered. We tell them that.
Do you integrate with our existing tools?+
Starter and Growth plans work standalone. No integrations required. The Business plan includes Gmail, Outlook, and Slack AI pre-fill, plus API access for HRIS and property management system integration. We're built so the core works immediately, on day one, without your IT team.
Is this a knowledge base like Trainual or Notion?+
No. Trainual, Notion, and Guru solve static documentation. Write it once, train from it. Lastday solves the dynamic, urgent, real-time moment of someone leaving mid-project with live tasks, active client relationships, and institutional knowledge that's never been written down anywhere. We're not a knowledge base. We're the emergency handover system you use when someone quits Friday.
Where is my data stored?+
Lastday is Canadian-built, Canadian-hosted, and Canadian-owned. Your data stays in Canada. No American servers, no American parent company, no American jurisdiction over your employee and business data. This matters more right now than it has in years.
How does the Lockdown feature work?+
Lockdown triggers automatically when a handover is marked Complete. It flags every active system access the departing employee held: licences, logins, tools, accounts. Your IT or operations team can close them immediately. 1 in 3 departed employees retain active access post-departure. Lockdown closes that door at the same moment the handover is finished.
What happens at the end of the free trial?+
At the end of the 14-day free trial, you'll be prompted to add billing details to continue using Lastday. If you choose not to subscribe, your account is paused and your data is retained for 90 days. No charges without your explicit action.
